Local Handyman Services

Reliable handyman services in Cook County and Gary

Go Direct Home Services provides professional handyman services for homeowners, renters, landlords, and property managers throughout Cook County, Gary, and Northwest Indiana. Whether you need furniture assembly, TV mounting, door repairs, or help with small home projects, we focus on clean work, clear communication, and dependable service.

Many customers reach out for help with everyday tasks such as assembling furniture after moving, mounting TVs securely, installing shelves, rods, and hooks, or fixing doors that don’t close properly. We also assist with blinds, curtain rods, cable management, and general home maintenance to keep your space organized and functional.

If you're searching for a local handyman near you, Go Direct Home Services makes it simple. Send your project details, and you’ll receive a free estimate with clear next steps. Small jobs are always welcome, and most estimate requests are answered the same day.
